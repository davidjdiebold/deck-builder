Deck Builder

Welcome to Deck Builder !

Deckbuilder is a software that helps you to find-out the best mana curve for your deck. You can configure your play-style in the Curve Builder view. The following options are available :

  • The turn of game where your deck will reach its maximum power. Use a low value for an aggro deck, or a high value for a control deck.
  • Computations precision. The more accurate, the more time to compute.
  • Hero Power to use in simulations. For example, Warlock card drawing abilities can be taken into account in simulations.

You can impose some cards in the deck if you want to ! Just add them to the forced cards list. The mana curve of your deck will be adjusted based on this information. Also, card drawing abilities from fixed cards will be considered when computing the best mana curve.

As soon as computations are ended, you will find the best mana curves for your deck in the Constructed Curves view.

You can also add some cards with given characteristics in the Library view, in order to use them in your deck. The most important things to consider when defining a card are the following :

  • The mana cost of the card.
  • The card rating is used by the software in order to compute deck scores. The contribution of a card to the score is proportional to its rating, and is increased if the card is played early.
  • Cards Drawn are used by the software when the card is played. The amount of cards is immediately drawn by the player.

When filling a deck with a given mana curve, Deck Builder fills mana costs with the cards with highest ratings. A card can be added twice to the deck.
